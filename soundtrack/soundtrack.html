Original Soundtrack

Welcome to the official Neopulse soundtrack section. Here you will find information about albums from various games. The musicians in this industry also bring video games to life!

Terraria

Over the life of the game, composer Scott Lloyd Shelly has produced hours of beloved soundtrack music, mixing chiptune with orchestral, rock, and other synthesized elements to create a bubbly and vibrant signature sound for Terraria.

Scott Lloyd Shelly, Re-Logic

Unravel

The soundtrack, created by Frida Johansson and Henrik Oja, is inspired by local folk music and traditional instruments and beautifully captures and enhances Unravel's mood and atmosphere. In Swedish it’s called “vemod” – best translated as “beautiful melancholy.”

Frida Johansson, Henrik Oja

Undertale

Undertale Soundtrack (stylized as UNDERTALE Soundtrack) is a soundtrack album by Toby Fox, released in 2015 for the video game Undertale.

Toby Fox

Blasphemous

Teleport yourself to the lands of Cvstodia and its different environments thanks to the original soundtrack of Blasphemous, composed by Carlos Viola. The album contains 32 original tracks from the game Blasphemous.

Carlos Viola

Poly Bridge

"Imagine going on the most relaxing road trip. From the Indie-hit video game Poly Bridge, let this soundtrack perfectly accompany you on your happy travels"

Adrian Talens

Hollow Knight

Christopher Larkin is an award winning composer and sound designer for games, film and TV. He has a number of achievements and collaborations under his belt, including the score and sound for indie game hit “Hollow Knight”, the orchestral score for feature documentary “Barbecue” and recent feature horror film "Awoken".

Christopher Larkin

Cuphead

The original soundtrack of Cuphead, composed by Kristofer Maddigan, is a fantastic jazz music collection that perfectly matches the game's 1930s cartoon style. Maddigan's compositions feature a mix of ragtime, swing, and big band jazz styles that capture the energy and excitement of the era. Overall, the Cuphead soundtrack is a must-listen for fans of jazz and video game music.

Kristofer Maddigan

To The Moon

To the Moon features a beautiful original soundtrack by Kan Gao, with additional music by Laura Shigihara, that perfectly captures the game's emotional and introspective themes. The music is a mix of piano-based compositions and orchestral arrangements that effectively immerse players in the game's world. Overall, the To the Moon soundtrack is a must-listen for fans of indie games and touching music.

Kan Gao, Laura Shigihara

Minecraft

The music of Minecraft, composed by C418, is a tranquil and ambient soundtrack that perfectly complements the game's peaceful and exploratory gameplay. The minimalist and repetitive tracks create a calming and immersive atmosphere that evokes a sense of wonder and discovery as players explore Minecraft's expansive world. The Minecraft soundtrack is a beloved and iconic aspect of the game that adds to its unique sense of relaxation and exploration.

C418
